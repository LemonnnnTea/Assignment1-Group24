Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my event?

We recommend booking at least 4-6 weeks in advance for smaller events and 8-12 weeks for larger events to ensure availability and proper planning.

What areas do you serve?

We serve the entire Tianjin area and can travel up to 50km from our location. Additional travel fees may apply for destinations beyond 25km.

Can I customize my party theme?

Absolutely! We specialize in custom themes. Our team will work with you to create a unique experience that matches your vision perfectly.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 30+ days before the event receive a full refund. 15-29 days: 50% refund. Less than 15 days: no refund but we can reschedule.
